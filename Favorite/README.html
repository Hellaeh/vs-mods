Usage:

  • Assign Modifier hotkey in settings
  • Click on item in your inventory with "(Hold)Modifier Key + Mouse Click"
  • You can change "FavoriteColor" in "helfavorite/ConfigClient.json" (default - #FFD000)

Mods that depend on this mod:

  • Quick Stack

Currently in alpha. Expect many bugs. Feel free to report any bug found in the comments below or on github


Known issues:

  • You have to restart the game after you change hotkey due to vanilla bug
  • Black bar on top of hotbar systematically appears, also somewhat poor performance - happen temp fix introduced in 0.3.0 until this issue is closed
